Hello! I am a recent Virginia Tech graduate with a degree in Political Science and Technical and Professional Writing. In my time at Tech, I learned a lot about the writing and research process, and I particularly enjoy editing.

I consider myself lucky to have been a writing coach at the Virginia Tech Writing Center as an undergrad. It gave me valuable experience in working directly with people on projects that ranged from creative writing assignments to doctorate dissertations. This experience is what has shown me my passion for helping people display their hard work in the best possible way.
